About

Silva Portentosa comprises texts, translations, and miscellanea by Brian J. Reilly (PhD, French, Yale University).

See the menu at the top right for its main projects:

  • Digital Bédier will provide an interactive text and translation of La tradition manuscrite du Lai de l’ombre by Joseph Bédier
  • French Studies will provide statistics about the history of French Studies in the United States of America

Until those projects' sites are ready, their content will be blogged about here first.

When not in the public domain, all texts are © 2018 Brian J. Reilly unless otherwise indicated.
